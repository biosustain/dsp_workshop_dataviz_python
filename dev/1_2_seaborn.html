Horizontal Bar Plot#

  • Using the orient parameter to switch to horizontal orientation.

  • calculates the mean and standard deviation of the mean automatically

ax = sns.barplot(
    data=df,
    orient="h",
    errorbar=("pi", 50),
    capsize=0.4,
    err_kws={"color": ".5", "linewidth": 2.5},
    linewidth=2.5,
    edgecolor=".5",
    facecolor=(0, 0, 0, 0),
)
